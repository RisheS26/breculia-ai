Privacy Policy — Emma (Home Services Vertical)

Last updated: April 2026

Emma is an intake tool only. She does not provide trade or emergency service execution.

1) What Emma collects

  • Caller name
  • Phone number
  • Service address
  • Job type
  • Urgency level
  • is_emergency flag
  • Call recording URL and transcript

2) How data is stored

  • Airtable for structured job records
  • n8n workflows (self-hosted on Mac M2)
  • Twilio for telephony and messaging flow

3) Sharing

Data is shared only with the home service contractor client using the system. No unrelated third-party data sale or sharing.

4) Retention

Calls and related records are retained for 90 days and then deleted, unless the client requests otherwise in writing.

5) General privacy rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, or export of relevant personal data by emailing founder@breculia.tech.
