Privacy Policy — Elena (Legal Vertical)

Last updated: April 2026

Elena is an intake tool only. She is not an attorney and does not provide legal advice.

1) What Elena collects

  • Caller name
  • Caller phone number
  • Case type
  • Language preference (Spanish/English)
  • Call recording URL
  • Call transcript

2) How data is stored

  • Airtable (structured intake records)
  • n8n workflows (self-hosted automation server) for routing/automation
  • Twilio for telephony and call handling

3) Sharing

Data is shared only with the law firm client that owns the intake channel. Breculia AI does not sell this data and does not share it with unrelated third parties.

4) Retention

Calls and related records are retained for 90 days and then deleted, unless the law firm client requests a different retention period in writing.

5) CCPA rights (California residents)

California residents may request access, correction, deletion, and information about data use. Requests can be sent to founder@breculia.tech.

6) HIPAA statement

Breculia AI and Elena are not covered entities and do not claim HIPAA compliance for this service.
