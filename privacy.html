Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026 · This policy explains how Breculia AI collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Who We Are

Breculia AI is operated by Rishe S(Founder). We provide AI-driven voice reception and automation services to business clients primarily in the Texas, Florida, Arizona, California, Nevada area and across the United States. Contact: founder@breculia.tech

2. Information We Collect

From business clients (B2B):

  • Business name, address, phone number, email
  • Owner and staff contact details for emergency escalation
  • Google Calendar access credentials (OAuth — we never store passwords)
  • CRM credentials (if integration is purchased)
  • Payment information (processed via Xflow — we do not store card details)

From callers (end customers of our clients):

  • Name, phone number, address, service request details
  • Call transcripts and recordings (stored in Airtable, accessible to the client)
  • Appointment booking data synchronized to the client's Google Calendar

From website visitors:

  • Standard web analytics (page views, referral source) via GitHub Pages
  • Google Calendar booking data when you schedule a demo

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and manage the AI voice receptionist service
  • To book appointments and send confirmations on behalf of clients
  • To send emergency alerts to client owners
  • To generate monthly performance reports for clients
  • To respond to support requests and onboarding communications
  • To invoice and process payments

We do not use caller data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Retell AI — Voice AI platform (processes call audio). Privacy Policy
  • Twilio — SMS delivery. Privacy Policy
  • Google Calendar API — Appointment booking. Privacy Policy
  • Airtable — Call log storage. Privacy Policy
  • n8n — Workflow automation (self-hosted)
  • Xflow — Payment processing. Privacy Policy

All third-party services are industry-standard and GDPR-aware. Caller data transmitted to these services is limited to what is necessary for service delivery.

5. Data Retention

  • Call transcripts and logs: retained for 12 months, then deleted unless client requests otherwise
  • Client business data: retained for the duration of the service agreement + 90 days post-cancellation
  • Upon written request, we provide all stored data in CSV format within 14 days
  • Upon written request, we delete all stored data within 30 days (except where legally required to retain)

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), OAuth for third-party integrations (no stored passwords), and access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the EU or California, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email founder@breculia.tech. We respond within 30 days.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: founder@breculia.tech
