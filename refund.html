Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Please read before purchasing any Breculia AI service.

Summary: Setup fees are non-refundable once deployment begins. Monthly retainers are refundable pro-rata only in cases of Breculia AI service failure. We stand behind our work — if we fail to deliver, we make it right.

1. One-Time Setup Fees

Setup fees cover custom build time, integration work, testing, and deployment of your AI voice receptionist system. Because this work begins immediately after payment:

  • Before deployment begins: Full refund available within 24 hours of payment if you request cancellation before we have started onboarding work.
  • After deployment begins: Setup fees are non-refundable. "Deployment begins" means we have started building your agent (typically within 24 hours of receiving your completed intake form).
  • If you paid but have not yet submitted your intake form, a full refund is available within 48 hours of payment.

2. Monthly Retainer Fees

  • Monthly retainers are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started.
  • Exception — Service Failure: If the AI agent experiences downtime exceeding 48 continuous hours due to an error on our part (not third-party platform outages), we will issue a pro-rata credit for the affected days to your next invoice.
  • Third-party platform outages (Retell AI, Twilio, Google Calendar) are outside our control and do not qualify for refunds. We will make best-effort attempts to restore service during such outages.

3. What Qualifies for a Refund

  • Setup fee cancellation within 24 hours of payment (before intake form submitted)
  • Pro-rata monthly credit for verified downtime exceeding 48 hours caused by Breculia AI
  • Duplicate payments — 100% refunded immediately

4. What Does Not Qualify for a Refund

  • Dissatisfaction with call volume or revenue outcomes (we cannot guarantee specific business results)
  • Third-party platform outages beyond our control
  • Client-caused issues (incorrect intake form data, failure to update business information, delays in granting access)
  • Change of mind after deployment has commenced

5. How to Request a Refund

Email founder@breculia.tech with subject line "Refund Request — [Your Business Name]" and a brief description of the issue. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days via the original payment method.

6. Disputes

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, contact us at founder@breculia.tech before initiating a chargeback. We resolve billing disputes fairly and promptly. Chargebacks initiated without prior communication will result in immediate service suspension and may affect eligibility for future services.
