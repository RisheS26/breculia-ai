Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026 · Please read before purchasing any Breculia AI service.

Summary: Setup fees are non-refundable once deployment begins. Early adopter pricing (March-April): $220/$500/$1,000. Regular pricing (after April 15): $2,500/$3,500/$5,000. Monthly retainers ($797/$1,097/$1,497) are refundable pro-rata only in cases of Breculia AI service failure.

1. One-Time Setup Fees

Setup fees cover custom build time, integration work, testing, and deployment of your AI voice receptionist system. Early adopter pricing available for NYC plumbers during March-April intake only. Because this work begins immediately after payment:

  • Before deployment begins: Full refund available within 24 hours of payment if you request cancellation before we have started onboarding work.
  • After deployment begins: Setup fees are non-refundable. "Deployment begins" means we have started building your agent (typically within 24 hours of receiving your completed Discovery Questionnaire or Onboarding Form).
  • If you paid but have not yet submitted your onboarding form, a full refund is available within 48 hours of payment.
  • Pricing tiers: Early adopter (March-April): $220 (Tier 1), $500 (Tier 2), $1,000 (Tier 3). Regular pricing after April 15: $2,500/$3,500/$5,000.

2. Monthly Retainer Fees

  • Monthly retainers are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started.
  • Exception — Service Failure: If the AI agent experiences downtime exceeding 48 continuous hours due to an error on our part (not third-party platform outages), we will issue a pro-rata credit for the affected days toward your next billing cycle.
  • Third-party platform outages (Retell AI, Twilio, Google Calendar) are outside our control and do not qualify for refunds. We will make best-effort attempts to restore service during such outages.
  • Cancellations must be submitted via email at least 7 days before the next billing cycle to avoid the subsequent month's retainer fee.

3. What Qualifies for a Refund

  • Setup fee cancellation within 24 hours of payment (before onboarding form submitted)
  • Pro-rata monthly credit for verified downtime exceeding 48 hours caused by Breculia AI
  • Duplicate payments — 100% refunded immediately

4. What Does Not Qualify for a Refund

  • Dissatisfaction with call volume or revenue outcomes (we cannot guarantee specific business results)
  • Third-party platform outages beyond our control
  • Client-caused issues (incorrect onboarding form data, failure to update business information, delays in granting access)
  • Change of mind after deployment has commenced

5. How to Request a Refund

Email founder@breculia.tech with subject line "Refund Request — [Your Business Name]" and a brief description of the issue. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days via the original payment method.

6. Disputes

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact us at founder@breculia.tech before initiating a chargeback. We strive to resolve all billing disputes fairly and promptly. Chargebacks initiated without prior communication will result in immediate service suspension and may affect future eligibility for services. Any bank or processing fees incurred due to a retracted or failed chargeback will be the responsibility of the client.
