Refund Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026 · Please read before purchasing any Breculia AI outbound operator service.

Summary: The $1,500 setup fee is non-refundable once build work begins (within 48h of payment). Monthly retainers (Managed $1,000/mo, Growth $2,500/mo) are non-refundable for the current billing period but cancel-anytime with 7 days notice. Month 2 is protected by our 30-Day Improvement Guarantee on the Managed plan. All payments via Xflow (ACH bank transfer).

1. One-Time Setup Fee ($1,500)

The setup fee covers real operator time and enrichment costs incurred on your behalf: Clay waterfall table construction, Apollo + Hunter + Prospeo enrichment across 500 leads, Instantly campaign configuration with domain warmup, AI-personalized opener writing, and a 20-minute Loom walkthrough delivery. Because this work begins within 48 hours of payment:

  • Setup fee is fully refundable only if Client cancels before any build work begins (i.e., before Breculia AI starts Clay table construction or Instantly campaign setup)
  • Setup fee is non-refundable once build work has started, even if Client later decides not to use the system
  • To request a pre-build cancellation refund, email founder@breculia.tech within 4 hours of payment with subject: Setup Cancellation — [Business Name]

2. Monthly Retainer Fees

  • Monthly retainers are non-refundable once a billing period has started
  • Cancellation: Submit written notice at least 7 days before next billing date to stop future charges. Service continues through the end of the current paid period.
  • Exception — Verified Service Failure: If Breculia AI fails to deliver the agreed weekly outputs (lead list refresh, copy updates, performance report) for 2+ consecutive weeks due to operator fault, a prorated credit is issued toward the next billing cycle
  • Platform outages on Clay, Instantly, Apollo, Hunter, or Prospeo are outside Breculia AI's control and do not qualify for refunds or credits

2A. 30-Day Improvement Guarantee (Managed Plan Only)

If the outbound system delivers fewer than 5 positive replies within the first 30 calendar days of campaign go-live, you may request Month 2 at no charge. To qualify:

  • Submit written request to founder@breculia.tech within 30 days of go-live date
  • Use subject line: Improvement Guarantee — [Business Name]
  • Client must have maintained active Clay Growth and Instantly accounts throughout the 30-day period
  • Low reply count must not result from Client changes to ICP, pausing campaigns, or domain configuration errors on Client's side
  • Breculia AI will verify Instantly campaign stats within 3 business days
  • If approved, Month 2 management fee is waived (applied as credit or invoice cancellation)

3. What Qualifies for a Refund or Credit

  • Setup fee cancellation before build work begins (within ~4 hours of payment)
  • Month 2 retainer waiver under the 30-Day Improvement Guarantee (Managed plan)
  • Prorated billing credit for verified consecutive operator-side delivery failure (2+ weeks)
  • Duplicate payments — 100% refunded immediately upon confirmation via Xflow

4. What Does Not Qualify for a Refund

  • Setup fee once Clay table build or Instantly campaign setup has begun
  • Monthly retainers for the current billing period (no refund for partial months or mid-cycle cancellations)
  • Low reply rates caused by ICP misalignment, saturated audiences, or market timing — these are external factors outside Breculia AI's control
  • Platform outages on Clay, Instantly, Apollo, Hunter, Prospeo, or Smartlead
  • Change of mind after delivery of system build and Loom walkthrough
  • Dissatisfaction with meeting volume or revenue outcomes — Breculia AI optimizes the system but does not guarantee specific business results

5. How to Request a Refund

Email founder@breculia.tech with subject line "Refund Request — [Your Business Name]" and include your Xflow transaction reference and a brief description of the issue. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days via Xflow ACH refund to the originating bank account.

6. Disputes & Chargebacks

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact founder@breculia.tech before initiating a dispute through your bank or Xflow. We resolve all billing disputes promptly — most within 2 business days.

ACH disputes initiated without prior written communication to Breculia AI will result in immediate service suspension. Any Xflow processing or reversal fees incurred due to a retracted, failed, or fraudulent ACH dispute are the responsibility of the client.
