Payment & billing

Flat USD pricing. No hidden fees. One payment method.

🏦

ACH bank transfer via Xflow

The only accepted payment method is ACH bank transfer, processed through Xflow. Xflow provides US virtual bank account details — you initiate the ACH transfer from your US bank account. No credit card, PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle. Clean, secure, no card processing fees.

Monthly retainers are billed on the same date each month as your first payment. Cancel with 7 days written notice before your next billing date — no partial-month refunds. The $1,500 Setup fee is fully refundable before any build work begins. See Refund Policy for full terms.
